Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 18, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. Safari Yatri collects and processes your personal data in accordance with this Privacy Policy and Nepal’s Privacy Act, 2075. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and your rights regarding your data.

Data Categories We Collect

We collect the following data to provide and improve our ride-sharing services:

  • Personal Information (Required at Registration): Name, phone number, address (to analyze user distribution), gender.
  • Personal Information (Optional): Email address, profile photo.
  • Account and Usage Data: Login ID, user type (Passenger/Rider Partner), wallet balance (for in-app ride payments), registration date/time, OTP sent date/time, activation date/time, login status, ride history, app interactions (via Firebase Analytics).
  • Location: Precise GPS location (foreground only, including current latitude/longitude and home latitude/longitude) to find nearby riders and provide navigation routing.
  • Crash Data: Device information and crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Driver Registration Data (for Rider Partners): National ID, driving license, vehicle bill book photo, selfie, vehicle owner name, vehicle owner phone number, vehicle registration number (to verify eligibility and ensure legal compliance).

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Facilitate ride-sharing services by matching Passengers with nearby Rider Partners.
  • Process in-app ride payments using the wallet system (via wallet balance).
  • Provide navigation routing via the Google Maps app (using URL launcher) and route optimization (via Google Maps API, LocationIQ).
  • Initiate phone calls via the device’s dialer app (using URL launcher) for user communication.
  • Analyze user distribution (using address data) to understand service coverage and improve operations.
  • Verify Rider Partner eligibility and ensure legal compliance (using driver registration data).
  • Analyze app usage (via Firebase Analytics) to enhance functionality.
  • Diagnose technical issues (via Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Ensure security, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data for marketing purposes. Data is shared only with:

  • Third-Party Services: Google Maps API and LocationIQ for navigation; Firebase Analytics for usage analysis; Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting; payment processors (if applicable) for wallet transactions.
  • External Apps: Phone numbers may be passed to the device’s dialer app for calls; location data (current/home latitude/longitude) may be passed to the Google Maps app for navigation, initiated via URL launcher with your consent.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights.

International Data Transfers

Your data, including wallet balance and driver registration data, may be processed or stored on servers located outside Nepal (e.g., via cPanel hosting, Google Cloud, or Firebase services) in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including Nepal’s Privacy Act, 2075.

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data, including wallet balance and driver registration data, from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Data Retention and Deletion

Upon account deactivation (soft delete), your account is flagged as 'terminated,' and data is retained for 1 month for security, fraud prevention, compliance, and user distribution analysis. You may request permanent deletion by emailing safariyatri1@gmail.com or submitting our Account Deletion Request Form. We will verify your identity and delete your data, including wallet balance and driver registration data, within 30 days, unless legally required to retain it.

Account Recovery

If your account is terminated, you will see a 'Your account is terminated' message upon login. To recover your account, contact us via safariyatri1@gmail.com or the Account Request Form. We will verify your identity before reactivating your account. Accounts are not reactivated without a valid user request.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. Users under 18 must use the app with guardian consent, as required by Nepalese law.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data, including wallet balance and driver registration data, subject to legal retention requirements. Contact us at safariyatri1@gmail.com for assistance.
